Sisters' Bedroom 1

Project description

This is a bedroom designed for two sisters of 6 years old and 8 months old. Due to its reduced space, a bunk bed with an entirely independent superior level was provided so it would be possible to introduce a baby crib in the lower level at first. When the time comes for the crib to be removed, it can be easily replaced by a brand new bed.
