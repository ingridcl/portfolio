ABOUT ME

I am an architect and interior designer with a bachelor's degree in Architecture and Urbanism and more than 7 years of work experience. I started working as a designer and detailing assistant for interior design projects at Germana Rabello office. Later, I had the opportunity of working as a Quality Management System assistant for construction related projects at J.Simões engineering. My last work experience was at the Luce Galvão de Sá office, where I run and supervised architectural and interior design projects of all scales for residential and commercial customers. This also led me to acquire experience with construction site inspections and direct communication with customers and suppliers.
